Global Payments Leadership Summit Announces Limited Release Of "Strategic Insight" Tickets At $3,999. Accessibility Excludes Insights.

*This content is not sponsored. That is part of what makes it more concerning.

Anne Thropic
CONFERENCES · April 16, 2026

The organisers of the upcoming Global Payments Leadership Summit have announced a limited release of discounted tickets, following what they described as "exceptional alignment across stakeholders".

Originally priced at $4,995, a small allocation of tickets is now available for $3,999, reflecting what organisers called a "commitment to accessibility".

Accessibility excludes travel, accommodation, meals, networking events, workshops, breakout sessions, side events, off-site dinners, breakfasts, coffees, and insights.

The two-day event promises an immersive experience for senior leaders across payments, fintech, banking and adjacent advisory roles, hosted at a luxury waterfront venue selected for its proximity to both the airport and executive ego.

What Attendees Can Expect

Keynotes from industry visionaries, including: a former executive who "scaled innovation" before moving into advisory; a consultant who helped write the strategy now being refreshed; a futurist who predicted the present with confidence; and a moderator described as "deeply embedded in the ecosystem".

Panel sessions include: Payments at a Crossroads: Why Now Is Different This Time; AI in Payments: Separating Signal From Noise (While Embracing Both); Cloud, Compliance, and Courage; and The Customer at the Centre, Somewhere.

Each panel will conclude with unanimous agreement that collaboration is critical.

No further action will be taken.

Premium Experiences

VIP ticket holders will enjoy: reserved seating near other VIPs; an invitation-only networking reception; a drink package aligned to the event's risk appetite; and the opportunity to be photographed in front of a branded backdrop.

All attendees will receive: a lanyard; a tote bag; access to slides uploaded six weeks later; and strategic clarity.

The strategic clarity is not guaranteed. A refund policy exists but requires documentation.

The Speaker Lineup

Speakers include: leaders who are "passionate about payments"; executives currently "on a journey"; founders who pivoted at the right time; regulators speaking in a personal capacity; and vendors speaking carefully.

Several speakers will appear on multiple panels to maximise thought-leadership efficiency.

"This is the most important gathering in the payments calendar. I say that at every gathering in the payments calendar. I mean it at all of them."

— Summit Co-Chair, who is also a keynote speaker, a panel moderator, and a sponsor.

Early Bird Conditions

The discounted rate applies until: tickets sell out; the price increases; or urgency is sufficiently established. A further discount may be released closer to the event, depending on registration volumes.

Registration volumes are described as "strong".

Attendees are encouraged to book early to avoid missing out on what organisers describe as "a critical conversation at a pivotal moment for the industry".

The pivotal moment will be revisited next year.
